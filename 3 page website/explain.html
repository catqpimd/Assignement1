Let's Get You Started!

You may be asking, "Hey, what the hell is a pomodoro?"

Well, fun fact: it actually means tomato in Italian!

(hence all the red theming)

The Pomodoro Technique involves breaking work into 25-minute intervals (pomodoros), followed by short 5-minute breaks. After 4-5 pomodoros, take a longer 15-30 minute break!

Try it out and see if it works for you!
